Learning Through Experience

Are you seeking a short, safe and impactful way to immerse your child in another culture? Do you desire for your teenager to improve his or her conversational English? Would your teenager like to experience America with American teenagers of their own age? Do you want to explore more of one of the safest, cleanest, most friendly and fastest growing cities in the United States?
Our Culture Immersion Program can help your family achieve these goals.

Our Specialty

Everyday Peer-to-Peer Interaction

Our staff includes American teenagers to lead or participate in small group discussion, assist in household activities, and participate in every tour or trip. Most of all, your child and American teens will build friendships with one another as they learn about each other’s culture.

Everyday Culture Experience

To enhance your child’s opportunity to learn American culture, we have carefully selected and screened American families to provide accommodations during the program. One American family will host two students and will do activities as a family with your child as part of the program.

Everyday English Conversation

Almost every day will begin with an English lesson developed around the activities of the day. After the lesson, students will divide into small groups for the discussion on the lesson.

Safety and Supervision

Adult staff will attend every event for maximum supervision. In case of medical problems, we have a registered nurse on staff at all times.

Location

Our organization is located in Austin, Texas, in the United States.
Texas offers everything that California and New York have: high tech industry from companies in Fortune 500 and nationally ranked universities.
Austin is rated as one of the safest, cleanest and fastest growing cities nationally for technology and job opportunities. Austin has a long history of establishing friendly international partnerships with the cities around the world, which promotes a welcoming environment for internationals.
