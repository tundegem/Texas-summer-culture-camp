Culture Immersion Program Details


1 Learn about American Education:
Tour top Universities and High Schools in Texas to experience a different educational system. Presentations and workshops are delivered on details of enrollment and schools.
2 Participate in Discussions:
Everyday small group discussions are organized around different topics to encourage maximum participation. Topics are selected reflectively from daily activities, such as Ethics, Values, Beliefs, Family, Career and more.
3 Think about the Future:
Tour Fortune 500 companies such as IBM, Dell Computer to watch how different professions work and begin forming ideas of what your dream career could be.
4 Care about the Environment:
Experience the state parks to see the preserves, visit the Texas coast, learn and hold some of Texas’ animals.
5 Splash into Summer Fun:
Ride horses on the beach like a cowboy or cowgirl, visit the biggest water park -- SeaWorld -- and other historical sites: these are just a few ways to enjoy the Texas summer.
6 Work as a Volunteer:
Participate in volunteering activities that help the poor or the environment to gain real work experiences in another culture.
7 Be Part of an American Family:
Live with an American family throughout the entire program to experience life in a different culture.
